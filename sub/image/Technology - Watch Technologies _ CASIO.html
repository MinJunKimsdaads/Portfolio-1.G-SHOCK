Innovation of Technology

The evolution of electronics
is expanding the realm of connectivity.
Contributing to liberation of our connected world.

Bluetooth®-equipped GPS Radio-controlled,
Solar-powered Timekeeping.

Learn More

GPS Hybrid Wave Ceptor

Casio proprietary time correction system provides constant access to the correct time at the current location in any time zone on the planet.

Smartphone Link

In addition to automatically adjusting the time via connection with a smartphone, the watch displays the time quickly in over 300 cities.

Multi Band 6

Terrestrial radio wave reception from 6 transmission stations worldwide. Automatic time correction available in Japan, North America, Europe and China.

World Time

Pursuit of original high-value operability and visibility, with an analogue dial to display World Time.

Tough Movement

This original slim movement has increased the reliability of CASIO’s radio-controlled, solar-powered analog watches further.

Tough Solar

CASIO’s original solar-charging system converts not only sunlight but also light from fluorescent lamps and other sources into power.

Smart Access

The evolution of the analogue chronograph is the story of combining multi-functionality with simple operation and display switching.

Triple G Resist

This innovative, further toughened G-SHOCK structure resists dropping shocks, centrifugal gravity and vibrations. Persistently advancing its evolution, G-SHOCK has broken through yet another boundary in the realm of toughness.

Shock Resist

The unique tough structure was born from an unprecedented idea. This technology has been passed down to every G-SHOCK model for over a quarter-century.

Triple Sensor, Ver.3

Three miniature sensors for measuring directions, barometric pressure / altitude, and temperature: These comprise PRO TREK’s core technologies for high-precision detection of sudden changes in natural phenomena.

Triple Sensor for FROGMAN

A dedicated Triple Sensor tuned specifically to FROGMAN’s specifications is installed to help divers meet their professional requirements.
